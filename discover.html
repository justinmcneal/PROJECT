THE PTC BUILDING
The PTC Building, where the College of Information Technology Education (CITE) and the Union Office are found, is located right above at the school's front entrance. The College of Law and its dedicated library are also situated in this building. The areas right next to this building are the PHINMA UPang Atrium, the Center for Student Development and Leadership (CSDL) Office, the Marketing Department, and the Information Technology Services (ITS) Office.
THE BE BUILDING
The Basic Education (BE) Building is where the following may be found: the Senior High School (SHS) Department and the BE Library on the 3rd floor, and the releasing area for student modules on the 1st floor. The areas right next to this building are the Nursing Building, the UPang gym, the Fidel V. Ramos (FVR) Library, and the PHINMA Avenue.
THE CAHS BUILDING
The Nursing building, located to the right of the BE Building, is where the College of Allied Health Sciences (CAHS) Department and the Ampitheatre are, both located on the 4th floor. Other areas right next to this building are the Upang Gymnasium, the Fidel V. Ramos (FVR) Library, and the PHINMA Avenue.
THE MBA BUILDING
The MBA building, named after Dr. Magdaleni B. Albarracin, is where the famous UPang logo is found. In this building you may find the Human Resources Department (HRD) Office, the College of Criminal Justice Education (CCJE), the College of Engineering and Architecture (CEA) Department, and the College of Education and Liberal Arts (CELA). The areas right next to this building are the North Hall Building, the UPang gym, the Student Plaza, the open field and old stage, and the PHINMA Avenue.
THE RIVERSIDE BUILDING
This newly-constructed building, located right behind the MBA Building, serves as a dedicated premise for students of medical-related programs, allowing them to have access to brand-new and state-of-the-art facilities catered for their needs in their practices. The College of Arts and Sciences Department has also been relocated on the third floor of this building.
THE NH BUILDING
The North Hall (NH) Building is where the SCL Office for College and the SCL Office for Senior High School are found, on the 2nd and 3rd floors respectively. The areas right next to this building are the CMA Building, the MBA Building, the open field and old stage, and the mini forest and pond.
THE CMA BUILDING
The College of Accountancy and Management (CMA) Building is where the CMA Department and the Banquet Hall are located, on the 3rd and 4th floors respectively. Beside the Banquet Hall are rooms dedicated for courses such as Hospitality Management and others which contain tools and equipements fit for their requirements. The areas right next to this building are the NH Building and the office of the Registrar.
OFFICE OF THE REGISTRAR
This office is where students may go to process different school-related documents and transactions. In the same area of this office are the Office of the Finance, the University Business Center, and the Office for Research. Right next to this office are the CMA Building and the Student Plaza.
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
This office is located between the office of the Registrar and the CMA hallway. Students may comein to process their different fees, permits, and scholarships here.
MARKETING DEPARTMENT
The Marketing Department, adjacent to the Center for Student Development and Leadership (CSDL) Office, is a place where all plans for school marketing objectives are discusses and created. Other areas located near to this office are the PTC Building and the office of the Registrar.
CSDL OFFICE
The Center for Student Development and Leadership (CSDL) Office is in-charge of keeping every student's well-being in check, focuses on improving their lives as students and as people, and handles different student affairs. This office located right after the main entrance of the university.
ITS OFFICE
The Information Technology Services (ITS) Department/office, aside from taking care of the school's Academic Information Management System (yan ba ung tamang ano ng AIMS?), offers services for making student IDs, and provides technical materials such as printer, television, cables, and more. The ITS Dept/office is located right above the CSDL Office and by the car entrance gate.
THE FVR BUILDING
The FVR Library, named after our former President Fidel V. Ramos, marks another special part of history at PHINMA- University of Pangasinan as they formally opened it on June 24, 2016. According to him, it was his first educational resource center and thats he hopes all people of central and Northern luzon can make use of it well. The FVR Library's first floor also serves as a bookstore. It is located right across the BE building and along the PHINMA Avenue.
GYMNASIUM
The UPang Gymnasium is an open and free space for all students to enjoy their sports. It also serves as a Physical education class space. In addition, it is where the teller's section, for queries regarding finance and fee payments, and the enrollment site for incoming students are found. The areas right next to the gymnasium are the MBA Building, the Nursing Building, the Student Plaza and the PHINMA Avenue.
STUDENT PLAZA
The Student Plaza has various food stalls where students are able to pick out from foods of many choices. This also has different booths put up during occasional univeristy events and festivities. Students use this space for practicing and holding busking performances. as well. The areas right next to the Student Plaza are the MBA Building, the mini forest, the office of the Registrar, and the PHINMA Avenue.
MINI FOREST
The mini-forest is an all-time favorite spot for students. The place gives a calm and peaceful vibe which lets students relax and enjoy the outdoor setting while studying. A mini pond is also located at a corner for people who are entertained by watching fish swim freely in the waters. There are seats and tables available for all. The pathways lead to the NH, MBA, and CMA Buildings. Other areas right next to the mini forest and pond are the Student Plaza, the open field, and the old stage.
